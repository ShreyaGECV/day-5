Famous place

Guru Nanak Jayanti, also known as Gurpurab, is one of the most sacred festivals for Sikhs, celebrating the birth of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, the founder of Sikhism and the first of the ten Sikh Gurus. In 2024, the occasion will be celebrated with immense devotion on November 15, marked by processions, kirtans, and a sense of unity. For those wishing to immerse themselves in the spiritual energy of this day, here is a list of ten renowned Gurudwaras in India that hold historical and spiritual significance.

The Golghar or Gol Ghar, is a large granary located to the west of the Gandhi Maidan in Patna, capital city of Bihar state, India.

Mandir is a Hindu temple dedicated to the god Hanuman, located in Patna, Bihar, India. Millions of pilgrims visit the temple every year. Acharya Kishore Kunal is the secretary of the Mahavir Mandir Temple Trust, Patna.

Bodh Gaya is a town in the Gaya district of Bihar, India. It's the place where Prince Siddhartha attained enlightenment and became the Buddha.
